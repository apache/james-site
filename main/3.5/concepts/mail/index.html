All About Emails

The history of email has resulted in a convoluted patchwork of standards and practices, which can be very difficult to understand and decipher. Fortunately, James has your back.

We have built up our own view of emails that allows us to process messages in a consistent and comprehensible way.

To help you understand how James approaches emails, we have divided this section up into the following parts:
